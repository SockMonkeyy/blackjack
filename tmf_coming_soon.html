What are Excess Funds?

When a property is sold at foreclosure or tax sale, it sometimes sells for more than what is owed. The extra money — called excess funds — may still belong to the former owner or other entitled parties.

Many people never realize this money is waiting to be claimed. We help locate, document, and recover those funds on your behalf.
